Queens University Belfast 2020 - 2024

1st Class Honours in Software Engineering with Digital Technology Partnership BEng

At Queens I undertook the Software Engineering with Digital Technology Partnership programme, also known as the Flying Start Degree programme.

This unique academic journey comprises a sequence of placements at PwC, including three summer placements and two six-month placements. These placements are split between Year 3 and Year 4, with Semester 2 of Year 3 spent at the university and Semester 1 of Year 4 spent at the university. The remainder of the time is dedicated to my placement at PwC. This unique structure allows me to gain hands-on experience at PwC while concurrently pursuing my BEng degree in Software Engineering.

For a more in-depth exploration of my experiences during my time at PwC, please refer to the Job History section.

Additionally, I am a proud member of the Queen's Computing Society (QCS) and the Google Developer Club. These extracurricular activities have afforded me the opportunity to delve deeper into various technologies and gain insights into professionals' experiences in the industry, which often extend beyond the standard curriculum at Queen's.

Within my time at Queens, I have actively participated in numerous hackathons organized by the school and various societies. These hackathons have covered a wide array of challenges, ranging from devising solutions for online learning to enhancing healthcare systems and deciphering ciphers. My involvement in these events has not only honed my teamwork skills but also enriched my understanding of technology and its real-world applications.
